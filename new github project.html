Md. Numeri Sattar Apar is a Bangladeshi educator and entrepreneur. A graduate in Civil Engineering from BUET (Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology), he co-founded Apar's Classroom, an online learning platform that has become popular among students in Bangladesh. Apar's Classroom, established in 2018, offers a variety of educational resources and courses, especially focusing on HSC (Higher Secondary Certificate) subjects like physics, with over 700,000 active students.
